# Made 400K Teaching Companies AI

In this episode, Matteo shares his success in generating over $400,000 through AI consulting and education, despite having no technical background.

## Core Thesis
The episode explores how individuals without a technical background can thrive in the AI industry by focusing on education and training. By fostering internal expertise and culture in companies, one can drive successful AI adoption and open additional avenues for services.

## Key Insights
- The AI agency space offers lucrative opportunities in consulting and training, especially for non-tech individuals.
- Internal cultural and operational changes are vital for successful AI adoption, not just technical solutions.
- Educating internal experts accelerates AI uptake and boosts product sales.
- Tailored workshops and a value ladder approach help capture client engagement and trust.

## Actionable Takeaways
1. Offer training as an entry service to build client relationships and pave the way for more comprehensive AI solutions.
2. Foster “AI champions” within client organizations to encourage ongoing AI adoption and integration.
3. Use live demos during training to effectively demonstrate AI values and capabilities.
4. Productize services to ensure scalability and recurrent revenue.

## Notable Quotes
> "AI wasn’t the problem; companies were. People inside the companies were."

> "Start lean, start fast with one clear offer that makes you a lot of money."
