# Ranked Every AI Side Hustle

In this episode, Liam Ottley delves into the world of AI side hustles, examining their viability based on his extensive experience with a community of over 230,000 AI entrepreneurs.

## Core Thesis
Liam Ottley argues that while 99% of AI side hustles are ineffective and offer little to no financial gain, there are select opportunities in this space that can lead to meaningful skill development and entrepreneurial success. Utilizing a 50-point evaluation framework, he assesses these side hustles on skills gained, earnings potential, client acquisition ease, long-term viability, and beginner-friendliness.

## Key Insights
- AI influencer creation offers significant skill development but challenges beginners due to creative demands.
- AI-powered drop shipping incurs high costs and low initial returns, making it unsuitable for newcomers.
- Meme marketing and AI Automation Agencies present innovative and promising side hustles.
- AI-assisted ideas are variably ranked—some high-potential, others not viable.

## Actionable Takeaways
1. Evaluate AI side hustles using five key criteria to ensure alignment with skills and goals.
2. Consider starting an AI Automation Agency, focusing on educating businesses about AI tools and implementing no-code solutions.
3. Leverage personal interests for sustaining motivation and avoiding burnout in AI endeavors.
4. Focus on high-potential fields like AI SEO and niche-vibe coding for novel website and app creation.
