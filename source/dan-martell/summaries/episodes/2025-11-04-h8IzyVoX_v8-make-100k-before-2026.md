# Make 100K Before 2026

Dan Martell shares insights and strategies for entrepreneurs aiming to significantly increase their income by 2026 by refining their business approach and client interactions.

## Core Thesis
To achieve financial success, entrepreneurs must adopt a scalable business model that focuses on delivering outcomes rather than charging by the hour. Simplifying service offerings, engaging effectively with affluent clients, and following a structured approach to sales and communication are critical to scaling businesses successfully.

## Key Insights
- Create a business model that prioritizes outcome-based pricing to facilitate scaling.
- Productize services for simplicity and focus on delivering a single, clear promise.
- Target wealthier clients who are more likely to appreciate and pay for valued outcomes.
- Use clear and concise offer sheets and proposals to prevent confusion and enhance credibility.
- Implement structured follow-up strategies to increase conversion rates.
- Leverage social media and direct engagement for client interactions.
- Handling objections effectively is crucial in the sales process.

## Actionable Takeaways
1. Design a business model focused on outcomes, not time.
2. Develop a productized service that solves specific client problems.
3. Use a structured follow-up system to maintain client engagement.
4. Engage with potential clients directly through social media and DMs.
5. Address client objections early and use a structured method to overcome them.
6. Focus on achieving "time to first value" to ensure immediate client satisfaction.
